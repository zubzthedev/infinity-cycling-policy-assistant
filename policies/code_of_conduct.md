# Code of Conduct

## Section 1: Expected Behaviour

Members are expected to treat other members, marshals, event officials, and
members of the public with courtesy and respect at all Club rides, races,
and social events.

## Section 2: Prohibited Conduct

The following conduct breaches this Code of Conduct and may be referred to
EXCO under the Disciplinary Policy:

2.1 Verbal abuse, shouting, or aggressive language directed at another
rider, marshal, or official.

2.2 Harassment, bullying, or intimidation, whether in person or via Club
communication channels (including group chats and social media).

2.3 Discrimination on the basis of gender, race, age, disability, sexual
orientation, or any other protected characteristic.

2.4 Riding under the influence of alcohol or drugs at a Club event.

## Section 3: Reporting

3.1 A member who experiences or witnesses a breach of this Code of Conduct
should report it to any EXCO member or the Secretary as soon as practicable,
in accordance with Disciplinary Policy Section 3.1.

## Section 4: Relationship to Other Policies

4.1 A breach of this Code of Conduct during a race or marshalled ride is also
subject to Racing Policy Section 2.3. Sanctions for any breach are determined
under the Disciplinary Policy.
