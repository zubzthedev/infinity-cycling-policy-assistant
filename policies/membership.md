# Membership Policy

## Section 1: Categories of Membership

1.1 The Club offers Full, Social, and Junior membership categories, each with
distinct fees and privileges as published annually by EXCO.

## Section 2: Rights and Responsibilities

2.1 Members are entitled to participate in Club rides, races, and social
events, and to vote at General Meetings.

2.2 Members must comply with the Constitution, the Code of Conduct, the
Racing Policy, and any lawful directions of EXCO or event marshals.

## Section 3: Suspension of Membership

3.1 EXCO may suspend a member's membership and riding privileges:

* as a sanction under Disciplinary Policy Section 3.4, following a hearing;
  or
* on an interim basis, for up to 30 days, where EXCO reasonably believes a
  member poses an immediate risk to the safety or wellbeing of others,
  pending a full disciplinary process.

3.2 A suspended member retains the right to appeal under Disciplinary Policy
Section 3.5.

## Section 4: Termination of Membership

4.1 Membership may be terminated by EXCO:

* as a sanction under Disciplinary Policy Section 3.4, following a hearing
  and any appeal;
* where a member fails to pay renewal fees within 60 days of the due date; or
* where a member resigns in writing to the Secretary.

4.2 A member whose membership is terminated as a disciplinary sanction may
not reapply for at least 12 months, and any reapplication is subject to
EXCO's discretion.

## Section 5: Non-Payment

5.1 Membership lapses automatically, without a disciplinary process, if
renewal fees remain unpaid 60 days after the due date. This is an
administrative lapse, not a disciplinary termination.
