# Constitution

## Section 1: Name and Purpose

1.1 This document is the Constitution of the Infinity Cycling Club ("the Club").

1.2 The Club exists to promote road and gravel cycling among its members
through organised group rides, races, and social events, and to govern its
own affairs in a fair, transparent, and accountable manner.

## Section 2: Membership

2.1 Membership is open to any individual who pays the annual subscription fee
and agrees to abide by the Club's Constitution, Code of Conduct, and other
official policies.

2.2 Membership categories, fees, and renewal terms are set annually by the
Executive Committee and published to all members.

## Section 6: Executive Committee (EXCO)

### 6.1 Composition

The Executive Committee ("EXCO") consists of the Chairperson, Vice-Chairperson,
Treasurer, Secretary, and up to four additional elected members. EXCO is
responsible for the day-to-day governance of the Club, including oversight of
the disciplinary process described in the Disciplinary Policy.

### 6.2 Voting and Conflicts of Interest

An EXCO member who has a direct or indirect personal, financial, or close
relational interest in a matter before the Committee must:

1. Declare that interest to the Committee before discussion of the matter
   begins;
2. Withdraw from the discussion and any vote on the matter, unless the
   remaining EXCO members unanimously agree that the member may remain for
   the discussion only; and
3. Not be counted toward quorum for the vote on that matter.

A vote taken in breach of this section may be declared invalid by a majority
of the unaffected EXCO members.

## Section 7: Amendments

7.1 This Constitution may only be amended by a two-thirds majority vote at a
General Meeting, following at least 14 days' written notice of the proposed
amendment to all members.
