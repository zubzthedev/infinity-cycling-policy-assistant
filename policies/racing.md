# Racing Policy

## Section 1: Scope

This policy governs the conduct of members during Club-organised races,
time trials, and marshalled group rides.

## Section 2: Race Conduct

2.1 Members must obey the instructions of race officials and marshals at all
times, including instructions to slow, stop, or take a specified line.

2.2 Dangerous riding, including but not limited to sudden unsignalled
braking, deliberate obstruction, or contact with another rider, is prohibited
and may be referred to EXCO under the Disciplinary Policy.

2.3 Abusive language or conduct directed at another rider, marshal, or
official during a race is treated as a breach of the Code of Conduct and may
also be referred to EXCO under the Disciplinary Policy.

## Section 3: Marshal Duties

3.1 Every Full member may be called upon to serve as a marshal at a Club
race or event, on a roster maintained by the Racing Secretary.

3.2 A member rostered as a marshal must attend or arrange a substitute at
least 48 hours in advance. Repeated unexplained non-attendance may be
referred to EXCO as a breach of member responsibilities under Membership
Policy Section 2.2.

3.3 Marshals have the authority to direct riders for safety purposes and
must report any safety incident or misconduct to the Race Director in
writing within 48 hours of the event.

## Section 4: Race Eligibility

4.1 A member under suspension pursuant to Membership Policy Section 3 is not
eligible to enter or participate in any Club race or marshalled ride for the
duration of the suspension.
